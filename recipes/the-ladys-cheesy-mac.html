The Lady's Cheesy Mac

Description

Paula Deen's famous Macaroni & Cheese recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons cut into pieces butter
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups grated cheddar cheese
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 4 cups cooked drained elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Once you have the macaroni cooked and drained, place in a large bowl and while still hot and add the cheddar.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the remaining ingredients and add to the macaroni mixture.
  3. Pour macaroni mixture into a casserole dish and bake for 30 to 45 minutes.
  4. Top with additional cheese if desired.
